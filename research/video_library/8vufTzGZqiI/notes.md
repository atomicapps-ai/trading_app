## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: On a 5m intraday chart, after the opening selloff. Daily trend bias must be bullish (price above 50-day SMA). The opening-range move (high→low from the bell) must exceed 20% of the daily ATR ("manipulation/sucker step"). Enter long when a candle takes out the prior candle's high (follow-through confirmation).
- exit/stop/target: Stop at the low of day; target = 50% of the opening-range move (peak-to-trough), optionally trail to the day's high.
- filters/params: ATR(14) daily for range; SMA50 daily for bias; mirror-image short side on weak assets (below 50 SMA).
