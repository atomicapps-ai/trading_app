## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: mark aged + visible liquidity levels (equal highs / equal lows = double tops/bottoms, prior-week/month highs, round numbers). When price sweeps through the level with a strong wick and wicks hard back inside the range, enter on the reversal ("confirmation candle") in the opposite direction of the sweep.
- exit/stop/target: stop just beyond the sweep extreme; target the middle of the range (or opposite side of the range).
- filters/params: level must be old + tested multiple times; timing filter (sweep should occur after 10:00/10:30 ET initial-balance, avoid 11:30-1:30 doldrums); trend filter via 34-EMA; align with higher-time-frame trend.
