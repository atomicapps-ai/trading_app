## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: This is a trend-DEFINITION lesson, not an entry strategy. Core rule: market is in a downtrend while price trades below the 50 EMA; trend flips to UP when (1) a candle prints fully above the 50 EMA with no wick touching it, then (2) a later candle closes above that candle's high. Mirror for down-flip (candle fully below 50 EMA, then close below its low). Between the two states = consolidation, sit out.
- exit/stop/target: None given. The video explicitly says trend ID "is not going to be the only thing you need" — entries come from separate pullback/breakout setups not specified here.
- filters/params: 50 EMA (author preference; says 20/100/200 also valid). Also offers a beginner variant: long-bias when 20 EMA > 50 EMA, short-bias when 20 EMA < 50 EMA.
