# bXOXc49IbCI — "Most accurate way to identify trend" (The Trading Channel)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bXOXc49IbCI>

## Rules (as described)
- A **trend-identification** method, not a full trade system. Downtrend while price is below the 50-EMA; a reversal is *flagged* only after (1) a candle closes above the 50-EMA without its wick touching it, then (2) a later candle closes above that candle's high, then (3) price breaks to new highs. Mirror logic for uptrend→downtrend. Between the flag and the breakout = "consolidation, sit on hands."
- Beginner alt: 20-EMA above 50-EMA = uptrend (take longs), below = downtrend (take shorts).

## Verdict: REJECT — not a strategy (regime filter only) + not mechanical end-to-end.
The video only teaches how to read trend direction/consolidation; it explicitly says this "is not the only thing you need" and defers actual entries to separate pullback/breakout videos. There is no defined entry trigger, stop, or target to backtest. It's forex-framed, and the usable kernel (moving-average trend/regime gate) is already baked into the live suite's SMA50/SMA200 filters (momentum_breakout, macd_run, fear_dip_reversion). Nothing new or testable as a standalone system.
Status: rejected
