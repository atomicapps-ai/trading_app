## Rules → hypotheses
1. **Big-body, no-wick candle = a real S/R level.** A large green (or red) candle with negligible
   wicks marks a price buyers/sellers defended; expect a bounce on retest. → **H-PA1**
2. **Mean reversion to the 50-period MA.** When price stretches far from the 50-MA, it tends to
   revert. → **H-PA2** (overlaps Lance H-MR1 and existing mean-reversion ideas)
3. **Momentum continuation.** Accelerating (steeper) moves tend to continue after a shallow pause. → **H-PA3**
4. **Pullback depth via Fib.** In an uptrend, a pullback that holds *above* the 0.382 retrace is
   "weak" (bullish, continuation likely); a pullback *below* 0.382 is "strong" (reversal/sideways). → **H-PA4**

Author's own caveat (notable): "any of these by themselves won't have a high win rate... combined,
or paired with indicators, it can be powerful." Honest — matches our confluence approach.
