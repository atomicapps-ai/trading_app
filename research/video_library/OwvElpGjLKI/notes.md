# OwvElpGjLKI — "How to backtest your strategy on TradingView" (tutorial)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OwvElpGjLKI>

## Content
A step-by-step tutorial on writing a Pine Script strategy and running the TradingView Strategy Tester. The only strategy shown is a throwaway demo — a 20-EMA / 50-EMA crossover (buy/sell on the cross) — used purely to illustrate the backtesting mechanics (strategy.entry / strategy.close, date filters, plot/overlay).

## Verdict: REJECT — a backtesting tutorial, not a strategy.
There is no genuine strategy proposal to validate; the 20/50-EMA crossover is a trivial illustrative example (and the video even states the entry/long conditions contradictorily). Simple MA-crossover systems are well known to be weak and are already covered by the project's moving-average research. Nothing new or mechanical to test.
Status: rejected
