   broken the *previous high*. Trend = sequence of valid higher-highs/higher-lows (up) or lower-

   retest, stop beyond the zone, target recent high/low. → **H-SD1**
3. **R:R filter ≥ 2.5:1.** Skip any setup whose stop/target geometry is under 2.5:1. → **H-RR1**
