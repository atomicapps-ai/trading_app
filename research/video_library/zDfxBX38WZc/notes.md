## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary candlestick reading, no system
It's an educational tour of reversal candles, not a mechanical strategy: no fixed entry trigger,
no stop, no target, and the key qualifier ("where you need to see them") is a discretionary
placement/context judgment. Isolated candlestick patterns are also weak signals on their own; the
mechanical reversal/mean-reversion edges are already validated/live (`fear_dip_reversion`, the
passed BB/RSI, BB-3SD, and Connors candidates). Nothing new and codeable to mine.
Status: rejected
