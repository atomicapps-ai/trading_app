## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: on a 2-minute chart, pre-select stocks where the 20-period and 200-period SMA are close together ("narrow/tight state"). If the first 2-min bar opens above the narrow MAs with an "elephant" bar (large solid green) or a bottoming-tail bar → buy when a later bar trades 1 tick above the first bar's high (long); mirror for shorts (open below, red elephant / topping tail). Also a "color game" add: after first red bar, add 1 tick above its high when a green bar takes it out.
- exit/stop/target: stop 1 tick below first bar (or above for shorts). Scale out over "three pushes." Trail stop under each fat bar / above green bars on shorts. Exit when state goes narrow→wide (MAs separate).
- filters/params: 2-min bars, SMA20 + SMA200, narrow-state filter, elephant/tail bar recognition, first-bar high/low breakout.
