# MCOiVEBjFnA — "QQQ Trading Strategy That Beats the Market" (Financial Wisdom / Masonson book)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MCOiVEBjFnA>

## Rules (as described) — all single-instrument / ETF timing & rotation
1. **225/200-day MA timing on QQQ:** long QQQ while price is above the 225-day MA, else cash.
2. **Seasonal switching:** hold Nov–June, cash Jul–Oct, entries/exits refined with MACD crossovers.
3. **Relative-strength rotation:** each month hold whichever of **TQQQ** (3× NASDAQ) or **TLT** (20y Treasuries) had the higher trailing 3-month return.
Plus a "weight of evidence" composite of 6 indicators (MAs, MACD, 7-period RSI, % of NDX above MA, Keltner, bullish-percent) — act when ≥3 agree.

## Verdict: REJECT — index/ETF timing & rotation overlays, out of the rig's scope.
Every strategy operates on a **single ETF or rotates between two ETFs** (QQQ / TQQQ / TLT), long-only, with no per-trade stop — a handful of regime switches or monthly rebalances, not the ≥~100 defined-risk R-multiple trades across the stock universe that the pass bar scores. Their documented value is drawdown reduction / allocation timing, not a high-PF trade edge. The MA-timing core (only be long above the long-term MA) is already embodied in the live suite's SPY>200-day-MA regime gate; the seasonal and dual-ETF-rotation pieces involve leveraged ETFs/bonds outside the core stock universe. Same conclusion as the Faber timing video (aenu7yUxTr8). Nothing new to adopt as a per-stock swing strategy.
Status: rejected
