## Content — discretionary supply/demand + zone refinement + MTF
- Demand zone = the last consolidation candle before a strong impulse up ("sideways candle with
  wicks, little movement, just before a large push"); supply zone = the mirror.
- "Sniper entries" come from **zone refinement** and multi-timeframe analysis (find the zone on a
  higher timeframe, refine it on a lower one).

## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary supply/demand, kernel already tested marginal
Which candle is "the" origin, how to refine the zone, and which timeframe to trust are all
discretionary. This is the supply/demand family whose mechanical version was already built and
tested in this project — `s4_supply_demand` (trend-aligned demand-zone retest) — and it came
back **marginal (PF ~1.04)**, below the bar. "Zone refinement" and MTF are exactly the
discretionary layers that can't be coded faithfully. No new, separable edge.
Status: rejected
