# LlHZil_7Ueg — "Break and retest" full strategy (~34 min)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LlHZil_7Ueg>

## Content — break-and-retest system
- Rejects SMC/ICT complexity; claims a simpler "break and retest" edge.
- Three higher-timeframe break-and-retest patterns + two intraday setups; a "displacement"
  entry criterion, with stops and targets.
- Mechanics: identify a level/zone, wait for a break with displacement, then enter on the
  **retest** of the broken level (frames show hand-drawn horizontal zones with resistance→support
  flips and retest entries).

## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary levels; kernel already covered
The edge rests on hand-drawn levels/zones (which swing, which zone, what counts as a valid
retest) — subjective and not reproducible bar-for-bar. The underlying mechanical idea, "break
of a level confirmed by a displacement/expansion candle, entered on continuation," is exactly
what the live daily strategies already encode deterministically: `momentum_breakout` (breakout +
volume/displacement confirm) and `coil_breakout` (contraction → expansion thrust). Entering on
the retest vs the break doesn't create a new, separable edge that can be faithfully coded from
discretionary zones. Same class as the other break/retest and trendline videos rejected here.
Status: rejected
