# 4cT8WTyxhYY — "Liquidity-Grab Reversal" (Trading Lab / Mr Double Your Money)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4cT8WTyxhYY> · ~10 min.

## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: After price breaks a support/resistance and immediately fakes back the
  other way ("liquidity grab"), enter at the candle that made the grab — long on
  a swept low, short on a swept high.
- exit/stop/target: Stop just beyond the liquidity-grab extreme; take-profit at the
  prior recent swing high/low (the opposite side of the range).
- filters/params: Bowski stat cited — first valley above second (true grab) beats the
  inverse. Optional confluences: peaks <10 bars apart give bigger drops; momentum-
  oscillator divergence; higher-time-frame S/R alignment (multi-TF); reversal must be
  at the top/bottom of a big move, not mid-trend.

## Verdict: ❌ REJECT — core depends on hand-drawn support/resistance + "fake-out" judgement, and the touted edge is multi-timeframe/15m+1h on Forex.
The S/R levels, "liquidity grab" recognition, and "is it at the top/bottom of a move"
gate are all discretionary; the demoed setups are 15m/1h FX which we have no data for,
and the swing analog (sweep of an N-day pivot low then reversal to the prior swing high)
is essentially the already-deployed Fear-Dip mean-reversion idea. No clean novel daily spec.
Status: rejected
