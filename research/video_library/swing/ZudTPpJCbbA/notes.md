# ZudTPpJCbbA — "Breakout trading secrets" (range breakout + momentum candle)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZudTPpJCbbA>

## Rules (as described) — daily-ish (generic charts)
- Draw support/resistance around a consolidation (wedge/flag/rectangle/triangle/pennant — all just "price consolidates between two levels then breaks out").
- **Entry:** only on a **momentum candle** breaking resistance — one large-bodied candle (majority of body beyond the level) OR three same-direction candles; enter at that candle's close (don't wait for a pullback).
- **Stop:** just below the resistance level. **Exit (2-step):** sell half at 1.5R, raise stop to the old TP, then trail the rest with the Chandelier stop (ATR ×2) and exit when it flips.
