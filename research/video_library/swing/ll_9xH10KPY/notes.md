# ll_9xH10KPY — "25% Retracement Scalping" (Trader DNA)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ll_9xH10KPY> · ~24 min.

## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: On a 1-min chart, trade with a "non-repaint" trend indicator (proprietary — red=short-only, green=long-only). Entry trigger = price crosses the 20-EMA back in the trend direction (closes below 20-EMA in red regime = sell; closes above in green regime = buy), confirmed by a Stochastic(8,5,3) crossover in the trade direction. Enter at signal-candle close or next-candle open.
- exit/stop/target: Target = only 25% of the "technical potential" of the pullback (scale to 50% optionally); stop "placed correctly" (unspecified). Claims 90% win rate at 25% target, 60% at 50%.
- filters/params: 1-min bars; 20-EMA; Stochastic(8,5,3); proprietary non-repaint regime indicator.

## Verdict: ❌ REJECT — pure 1-minute scalping built around a proprietary closed-source "non-repaint" indicator; the central thesis (tiny 25% targets → 90% WR) is a money-management framing with no defined stop, not a testable edge, and the regime signal is unavailable.
The only mechanical leftover (20-EMA reclaim + Stochastic crossover) is an intraday mean-reversion scalp; it does not survive translation to daily bars and duplicates nothing useful. No salvageable novel daily core.
Status: rejected
