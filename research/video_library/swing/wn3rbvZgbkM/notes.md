# wn3rbvZgbkM — "How to find high-quality setups (significant volume / gaps)" (forex)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wn3rbvZgbkM>

## Content
A concept (not a full strategy): mark where "significant/major volume" sits (swing highs/lows) and the "unfilled gaps" either side; the high-probability trade is when price sweeps a gap into major volume and reverses, while the choppy area in between is "noise/traps." Demoed on forex (EUR/USD, EUR/CAD) on 15m/5m; explicitly requires a higher-timeframe narrative to be tradable.

## Verdict: REJECT — forex, intraday, discretionary concept.
Not a codifiable system — the author presents it as an isolated concept that only works layered with a discretionary higher-timeframe narrative; identifying "significant volume" and "unfilled gaps" is subjective. Forex pairs on 15m/5m intraday, not daily US stocks. No objective entry/stop/target to backtest.
Status: rejected
