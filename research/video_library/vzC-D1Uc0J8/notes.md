## Content — trendline continuation + breakout patterns
- Draw trendlines as "key levels" in a trending market. Two patterns when price reaches the line:
  a **continuation** (bounce back in trend direction) or a **breakout** (line breaks, trend
  switches). The video notes a touch doesn't guarantee a reaction — you need extra discretionary
  price-action confirmation.
