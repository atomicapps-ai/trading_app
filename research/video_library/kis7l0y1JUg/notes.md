## Rules (as described)
- Two timeframes: 1h for structure, 15m for entries. Identify trend (HH/HL). Wait for price to return to a hand-drawn key level (resistance/support), then look for a "momentum shift" — big candles losing steam, long-wick rejection candles, a break of a small structure level — and enter on a confirmation candle close. Stop beyond the level; target 2–3R.
