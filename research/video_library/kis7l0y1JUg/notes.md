## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: Wait for price to retrace to a hand-drawn "key level" (support/resistance), drop to a lower time frame (15m), and enter on a momentum-shift candle (long-wick rejection / close beyond a manually-drawn micro level / "break of structure").
- exit/stop/target: Stop above/below the drawn swing level; take-profit at 2R or 3R ("I just like to go for three times").
- filters/params: HTF (1h/50m) trend bias via eyeballed higher-highs/higher-lows; no numeric indicator params. Multi-timeframe (1h context + 15m entry).
