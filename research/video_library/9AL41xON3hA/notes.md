# 9AL41xON3hA — "The Fractal Model" (TTrades / ICT-style multi-timeframe)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9AL41xON3hA>

## Content — discretionary multi-timeframe swing/POI model
- **Daily bias:** classify the daily close — outside the prior-day range → continuation;
  sweep-and-reclaim of prior-day high/low → reversal.
- **Structure + swing points:** drop to lower timeframes, wait for a bullish/bearish **swing
  point** to form **at a point of interest**, with specific **candle closures**, then
  anticipate the following **expansion candle**.
- Entry, POI selection, and confirmation are all read by eye on 5-min structure (frames show
  labelled swing points C2/C3 and hand-placed POI zones).

## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary ICT model
The core signal — "a swing point forming at a point of interest with the right candle closure"
— is a discretionary read: which level is a valid POI, which swing counts, and which closure
confirms are all judgment calls with no numeric definition. This is the same ICT/multi-TF
family already rejected repeatedly in this run (fractal/POI/expansion, no defined stop or
target). The one crisp, mechanical piece — "daily close outside the prior-day range →
continuation next day" — is only a *bias filter*, not a complete strategy, and its
continuation flavor is already embodied by the live `momentum_breakout` / `coil_breakout`
daily strategies. Nothing new and mechanizable to test.
Status: rejected
