# R2hZlnh37fQ — "Simple pullback strategy" (price action)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R2hZlnh37fQ>

## Rules (as described)
- Pullback ends at one of three levels: flipped support/resistance, a trendline, or the 50-period MA; prefer areas of confluence (multiple levels overlapping).
- Enter method 1: at the confluence, on a reversal candlestick (bullish/bearish engulfing, dojis). Method 2: draw a small counter-trendline on the pullback and enter on its break. Stop beyond the level; target the prior S/R.
