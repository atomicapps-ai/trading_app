# R2hZlnh37fQ — "Simple Pullback Strategy" (Trading Geek)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R2hZlnh37fQ> · ~9 min.

## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: In an existing trend, wait for a pullback to one of three "confluence" levels — prior support/resistance flip, a hand-drawn trendline, or the 50-period MA. Enter when (a) a reversal candlestick (bullish/bearish engulfing, doji cluster) prints at the confluence area, OR (b) price breaks a hand-drawn counter-trendline of the pullback.
- exit/stop/target: stop above last lower high / above trendline (or below last higher low); take profit at the next S/R level.
- filters/params: 50-period MA, hand-drawn trendlines, S/R flip zones, engulfing/doji candlestick confirmation. "Increase lot size" at strong confluence.

## Verdict: ❌ REJECT — pure discretion (hand-drawn trendlines + visual confluence + candlestick judgment)
The strategy explicitly hinges on drawing trendlines, counter-trendlines, and S/R zones by eye and judging "areas of confluence" plus candlestick patterns — all non-mechanical. The only objective ingredient (50MA pullback in a trend) is a generic trend-continuation idea that isn't novel against our deployed Momentum Breakout. No clean mechanical spec survives.
Status: rejected, not deployed
