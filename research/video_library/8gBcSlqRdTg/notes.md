# 8gBcSlqRdTg — "Boring/mechanical Break-and-Retest" (intraday options scalper)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8gBcSlqRdTg>

## Rules (as described)
- **Instrument:** intraday **options** (0DTE/short-dated puts & calls on TSLA, NVDA, QQQ). **Timeframes:** 1-minute / 5-minute.
- **System:** "break and retest" at key levels, three variants — (1) opening-range break-and-retest (mark first NY-open candle high/low, trade the break then retest); (2) order-block retest (up-close candle as resistance in a downtrend, etc.); (3) previous-day high/low break-and-retest.
- **Entry:** market order on the retest reaction; **stop:** just beyond the level; **target:** next intraday level / partials; discretionary higher-timeframe "context and logic" gates every trade.

## Verdict: REJECT — out of scope.
This is discretionary **intraday options day-trading/scalping** on 1-min/5-min charts (the author's own P&L screenshots are option-contract fills). Not daily US-stock swing trading. The setups are keyed to intraday session structure (opening range, previous-day levels) and depend heavily on real-time discretion and options mechanics — nothing representable in the project's daily backtest rig.
