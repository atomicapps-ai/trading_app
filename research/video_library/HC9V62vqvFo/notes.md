# HC9V62vqvFo — "High Probability Trading Setups" (Netpicks)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HC9V62vqvFo> · ~11 min.

## Rules (mechanical)
- trend filter: 30-period SMA. Trend = slope of SMA + structure: for an uptrend, need two higher lows plotting fully above the SMA, confirmed when price breaks the highest high of those two candles (mirror for downtrend). Trade only in trend direction.
- setups (4): (1) pre-range breakout — buy a base/small range forming just under resistance in an uptrend, entering before the breakout; (2) reversal off pullback — draw a trendline through the pullback candles, enter on trendline break, only when a momentum indicator slopes up; (3) failure test of highs/lows — price breaks a swing low then wicks back (lower shadow), enter on break of the reversal/inside candle; (4) triangle (inside-bar) break — 4-point triangle, enter on trendline break in trend direction.
- exit/stop/target: not specified ("determine your own risk management / profit targets").

## Verdict: ❌ REJECT — tutorial overview of four discretionary setups with hand-drawn trendlines, "obvious swing points," and no defined exits/targets; examples span futures/crypto/intraday, and the one mechanical piece (SMA30 + 2-higher-lows trend confirmation) is a weaker variant of our deployed momentum/breakout trend gates.
Setups 2 and 4 require hand-drawn trendlines (explicitly non-mechanical); the video gives no stop or target rules and openly defers them to the trader. The SMA30 swing-structure trend filter is testable but is just a trend gate, not a complete novel strategy, and overlaps our existing momentum-breakout regime gate.
Status: rejected
