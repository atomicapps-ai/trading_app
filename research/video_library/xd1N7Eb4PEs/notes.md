Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xd1N7Eb4PEs>

## Content — discretionary swing trades at S/R
- Swing trading = capture one big swing and exit before it reverses. Buy at a support level,
  target the next resistance (and mirror for shorts). Strategy #1 "catch the wave" = ride one
  move in a trending market; the rest hang off buying support / selling resistance.

## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary support/resistance
Every setup is anchored on hand-picked support/resistance levels (which prior swing counts as the
level is a judgment call) with entry/exit read off them — no numeric rule. This is the
discretionary S/R family already rejected in this run. The mechanical analogs — mean-reversion
bounce from an extreme and trend-continuation — are already validated/live (`fear_dip_reversion`,
`momentum_breakout`, and the passed mean-reversion candidates). Nothing new and codeable.
Status: rejected
