## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary zone/liquidity concepts
Every setup rests on hand-drawn "obvious" levels / supply-demand zones and a discretionary read
of the sweep or rejection — no numeric entry/stop/target. The lead setup is the ICT stop-hunt
liquidity concept already rejected repeatedly in this run (and its mechanical sweep-reversal
cousin tested marginal). The zone-rejection setups are discretionary supply/demand, whose
validated mechanical analogs (mean-reversion from an extreme; contraction→breakout) are already
live. Gold-specific packaging doesn't add a new, codeable edge.
