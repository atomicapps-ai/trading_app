# cKKLujAdvzk — "Simple pullback strategy" (3 levels + confirmations)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cKKLujAdvzk>

## Rules (as described)
- **Pullback ends at one of three levels:** flipped support/resistance, a trendline, or a moving average (20/50/100/200 — "adjust to whatever the market is reacting to"). Prefer areas of confluence (level + MA).
- **Confirmation (pick one):** rejection candlestick (bullish/bearish engulfing, hammer); break of a small counter-trendline drawn on the pullback; or RSI (set to 50/50 = single mid-line) crossing back through the middle.
- Enter with the trend after confirmation; examples include **USD/CAD** (forex).

## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary + duplicate kernel.
Level identification is subjective (draw S/R, draw trendlines, "adjust the MA to what the market is reacting to") and each confirmation is a judgment call, so it's not objectively mechanical. Multi-asset/forex-framed. The one codifiable core — pull back to the 50-MA in a trend, then enter on confirmation — is exactly what was already mechanized for video AKkeB8RJ6jM (result: OOS PF ~1.0, break-even) and is covered by the live `fear_dip_reversion`. No new testable edge.
