# cKKLujAdvzk — Pullback strategy (Data Trader)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cKKLujAdvzk> · ~10 min, daily.

## Rules
Buy pullbacks in a trend to one of: support/resistance, a trendline, or a moving average (50-period
default), confirmed by EITHER a candlestick pattern (engulfing/hammer), a small-trendline break, or
RSI crossing its 50 midline. Stop beyond the level; trade in trend direction.

## Verdict: ❌ REJECT — discretionary + overlaps tested pullback
The core is "buy a pullback to the 50-MA on a reversal," but the method is largely **discretionary**
(hand-draw trendlines, pick "areas of confluence," choose which confirmation to use). Its mechanical
core ≈ the **20-EMA pullback we already backtested (+0.04R, PF 1.06 — marginal)**. No new edge to add.
Status: rejected, not deployed.
