Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5X_ZcifasBg> · ~18 min.

## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: Scan for the day's leading percentage gappers (low-float small caps, $2–$20 price, <20M float, gapping up on news/catalyst). Buy the first intraday pullback to the 9-EMA / VWAP on the 1-min or 5-min chart as the candle makes a new high.
- exit/stop/target: discretionary intraday scalp ("get in green, get out"); avoid 2nd/3rd pullbacks and topping tails; flat by end of day.
- filters/params: float <20M, price <$20, news catalyst, VWAP, 9-EMA, 1-min/5-min/10-sec timeframes.
