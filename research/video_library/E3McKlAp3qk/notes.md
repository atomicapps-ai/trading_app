# E3McKlAp3qk — "Displacement ORB + Session Analysis" (Trade with Pat)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E3McKlAp3qk> · ~12 min.

## Rules (mechanical)
- entry: New York open. Mark the 9:30–9:45 ET 15-min opening range (high/low). On 5-min,
  wait for an impulsive "displacement" candle close out of the range that creates a demand
  zone / fair-value gap; enter long when price retraces to that zone and prints a bullish
  engulfing (or simply holds the zone).
- exit/stop/target: Stop below the demand zone (or FVG midline); target ~1.5–2.2 R, often
  the swept Asian-range liquidity level.
- filters/params: Session bias — Asian ranges, London pushes, NY reverses; trade NY in the
  opposite direction to the London push. If London sweeps BOTH sides of the Asian range,
  expect continuation, not reversal.
