# qLtq73bTPBA — "Trendline Break" bounce playbook (~55 min, Tori Trades)

Source: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qLtq73bTPBA>

## Content — trendline break-and-retest ("holy grail")
- Draw a trendline (the "action line"); a parallel "safety line" holds the stop.
- Trade the **break and retest** / bounce of the trendline; drag the stop to the far side of the
  safety line; same setup/criteria across instruments and timeframes ("this is the holy grail").

## Verdict: REJECT — discretionary trendline
Everything is anchored on a hand-drawn trendline (which swings to connect, what slope, how many
touches) — subjective and non-reproducible, so there's no deterministic break, retest, entry, or
stop. Same discretionary trendline family already rejected in this run (FjxG1-dGzyU,
G29LbG1Xkvw). The mechanizable cousin (break of a rolling swing structure) is already live via
`momentum_breakout` / `coil_breakout`. Length and enthusiasm don't make it codeable.
Status: rejected
